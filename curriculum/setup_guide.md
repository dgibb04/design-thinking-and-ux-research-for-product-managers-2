# Environment Setup Guide

## 1. Room Setup
- **Layout:** U-shaped table arrangement to facilitate group discussion and pair work [Source: tmpstix5vzg.docx].
- **Whiteboards:** Place at the front for facilitator use; provide wall space for post-it mapping.

## 2. Miro Board Setup
- Create a master board with sections: 'Research Planning', 'Affinity Mapping', 'Ideation', 'Prototyping', 'Feedback Grid'.
- Use templates provided in the Miro library for 'Affinity Mapping' and 'User Journey' [Source: tmpisnh0if3.docx].

## 3. Pre-printed Materials Checklist
- 12 x Workshop Agendas.
- 12 x Interview Guide Templates.
- 12 x Persona Cards.
- 12 x Feedback Capture Templates.
- 10 x Post-it pads (Backup for bandwidth issues).

## 4. Troubleshooting & Offline Fallback
- **Wi-Fi Failure:** If the 30 Mbps connection fails, immediately pivot to physical paper prototyping and physical post-it mapping [Source: tmpisnh0if3.docx].
- **Browser Issues:** Ensure all participants have Chrome or Edge installed; no specific VPN is required for the coworking space [Source: Unknown].

## 5. Day-of Timeline
- **08:00:** Arrive, set up tables, distribute printed materials.
- **08:30:** Test projector and Wi-Fi connectivity.
- **08:45:** Welcome participants.
